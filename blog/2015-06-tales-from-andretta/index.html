Tales from Andretta

A workshop held at NCFA, Woodlands Society Andretta from 6th-9th June 2015 by Urvi Khanna for children on story making. A small group of children from 5 to 14 years participated with great enthusiasm over the four days ending with a performance on the last day.

We started with stories they knew and moved on to stories from their own experiences of school, travels, desires, fears and joys. Working at NCFA is a special experience, view of the mountains, cool breeze, bird sounds, happiness!..
